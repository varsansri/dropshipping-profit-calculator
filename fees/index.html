All Dropshipping Fees in 2024 — Complete Cost Breakdown

Before you run a single ad, you need to know every fee that will eat your margin. Most dropshippers only calculate product markup and forget 6 other cost categories.

The 7 Fee Categories Every Dropshipper Faces

1. Platform / Storefront Fee
$15–$399/month
Shopify Basic = $39/mo. WooCommerce + hosting = ~$20/mo. TikTok Shop = Free (2024). Factor this into your per-unit overhead based on monthly order volume.
2. Transaction Fee
0–2% per order
Shopify charges 2% on Basic if you don't use Shopify Payments. Goes to 0.5% on Advanced. WooCommerce with Stripe = 0% platform fee. Use Shopify Payments to eliminate this fee entirely.
3. Payment Processing Fee
2.4–3.5% + $0.30
Stripe and Shopify Payments: 2.9% + $0.30 (Basic), 2.5% (Advanced). PayPal: 3.49% + $0.49. On a $50 order, this is $1.75–$2.25 per transaction.
4. Supplier / Product Cost
20–60% of selling price
AliExpress: typically cheapest but 2–4 week shipping. CJ Dropshipping and Zendrop offer faster shipping at higher cost. Always get the true landed cost including shipping.
5. Advertising / Customer Acquisition
$15–$60 per customer (CPA)
The biggest variable. Facebook Ads CPA varies enormously by niche. Beauty: $15–25. Home goods: $20–40. Health: $25–50. Calculate break-even CPA before launching.
6. Returns & Refunds
2–15% of revenue
Average e-commerce return rate is 20–30%. Dropshipping gets lower returns (5–15%) due to product type, but refund costs include lost product, return shipping, and processing fees.
7. Chargebacks
0.5–2% of revenue
Chargebacks cost the selling price + a chargeback fee ($15–25 per dispute). High chargeback rates can result in your payment processor shutting down your account. Always have a clear refund policy.
