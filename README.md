# Dropshipping True Profit Calculator
> Most dropshippers think they make 30% margin. They're making 4%. Find out your real number.

**[Live Demo →](https://varsansri.github.io/dropshipping-profit-calculator)**

## What It Does
Enter selling price, product cost, shipping, platform fee %, payment processing, ad spend per order, and returns rate. The tool calculates your true profit per order, true margin %, ROI, and shows a visual cost bar breaking down exactly where your revenue goes. Shows how many orders you need to hit $1,000/month.

## Who It's For
- New dropshippers validating product viability
- Existing dropshippers suspecting their margins are worse than they think
- Shopify/Amazon/TikTok Shop sellers running Facebook or TikTok ads
- Ecommerce entrepreneurs choosing between product pricing strategies
